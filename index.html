BACKGROUND

Marvenn Academy foundation was laid by Dr. Vikrant and Dr. Nitu Singh, who obtained their Doctorate degree from Tata Cancer Hospital (India’s largest and most prestigious Cancer Treatment and Research Centre), and were associated with Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC), IIT Bombay and AIIMS Delhi. Marvenn institute was born to unleash the potential of Medical Science in the preparation of present and future doctors/medicos (Pre-medical, MBBS/MD, Para-medical, Pharmacy/PG Medical entrance aspirant and academics).
